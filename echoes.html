WuWa Echo Tier List - Best Sets & Main Stats

Complete echo database with set bonuses, main stat priorities, and optimized farming routes for every build.

Echoes are the gear system in Wuthering Waves - each character equips 5 echoes that provide stats and set bonuses. Getting the right main stats is more important than perfect substats. A character with correct main stats and mediocre substats will outperform one with perfect substats but wrong main stats. This guide covers the optimal setup for every role.

Top Meta Sets

Celestial Light (5pc)

2pc: Spectro DMG +10%. 5pc: Spectro DMG +30%. After casting Resonance Skill, gain additional 18% Spectro DMG for 15s. Best for: Jinhsi, Phoebe. This set is mandatory for Spectro DPS - the 48% total Spectro DMG bonus is unmatched.

Sun-sinking Eclipse (5pc)

2pc: Havoc DMG +10%. 5pc: Havoc DMG +30%. After casting Intro Skill, gain 48% Havoc DMG for 15s. Best for: Camellya, Roccia. The Intro Skill trigger makes this perfect for quickswap Havoc teams.

Molten Rift (5pc)

2pc: Fusion DMG +10%. 5pc: Fusion DMG +30%. After casting Resonance Skill, Fusion DMG +18% for 15s. Best for: Changli, Brant, Encore. Standard elemental set, reliable and easy to farm.

Void Thunder (5pc)

2pc: Electro DMG +10%. 5pc: Electro DMG +30%. After casting Heavy Attack, Electro DMG +18% for 15s. Best for: Xiangli Yao. Heavy Attack trigger requires specific rotations but rewards with high uptime.

Freezing Frost (5pc)

2pc: Glacio DMG +10%. 5pc: Glacio DMG +30%. After casting Resonance Skill, Glacio DMG +18% for 15s. Best for: Zhezhi, Sanhua. Standard elemental set for Glacio characters.

Support Sets

Rejuvenating Glow (5pc)

2pc: Healing Bonus +10%. 5pc: Healing Bonus +20%. After healing, grant team 15% ATK for 20s. Best for: Shorekeeper, Verina. The universal support set. Always put this on your healer - the team ATK buff is essential.

Moonlit Clouds (5pc)

2pc: Energy Regen +10%. 5pc: Energy Regen +20%. After casting Outro Skill, grant next character 22.5% ATK for 15s. Best for: Sub-DPS supports. Great on characters you swap out quickly after using their skills.

Echo Farming Tips

  • Farm 4-cost echoes first - they drop from weekly bosses and have the biggest impact on damage.
  • Data Bank level 20 unlocks gold echo drops. Prioritize leveling your Data Bank by absorbing every new echo.
  • Tacet Fields are the most efficient farming spots - they drop echoes of a specific set with guaranteed rarity.
  • Don't chase perfect substats early. Correct main stats + set bonus = 80% of max potential. Substats are the last 20%.
  • Merge bad echoes using the Data Merge feature to recycle unwanted gold echoes into new ones.
