🎯 Main Stat Guide (Per Slot)

🔴 Cost 4 — CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG

DPS always CRIT. Healers use HP% or Healing Bonus.

🟡 Cost 3 ×2 — Element DMG%

Match character element (e.g. Fusion DMG for Changli). Supports use Energy Regen%.

🔵 Cost 1 ×2 — ATK%

Universal for DPS. Supports use HP% or DEF% depending on scaling.

🏗️ 43311 vs 44111

43311 (Standard)

1× Cost 4 + 2× Cost 3 + 2× Cost 1. Recommended for most DPS. Gives 2 Element DMG% slots for max damage. Flexible and easiest to farm.

44111 (Alternative)

2× Cost 4 + 3× Cost 1. Higher raw stats but less Element DMG%. Viable for characters with built-in DMG% buffs (e.g. Jinhsi with sequences). Harder to get perfect CRIT substats.
