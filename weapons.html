WuWa Weapons Tier List - Best Weapons Ranked

Every 5-star and notable 4-star weapon ranked by type. Includes passive effects, best character matches, and F2P alternatives.

Weapons in Wuthering Waves are character-locked by type - each Resonator can only equip their specific weapon category. This guide covers the best-in-slot weapon for every character, plus budget alternatives that perform nearly as well. Weapon passives are often more important than base stats, so read carefully before pulling.

Sword (5-star)

Ages of Harvest

Best: Jinhsi - CRIT Rate 24.3%

Passive: Increases all Element DMG by 12-24%. After casting Resonance Skill, gain 7-14% ATK per second (max 5 stacks). Jinhsi's signature.

S

Best: Camellya - ATK% 36.4%

Passive: Basic Attack DMG +20-40%. When HP is below 50%, gain additional 15-30% Havoc DMG. Camellya's signature.

S

Standard - CRIT Rate 24.3%

Passive: ATK +12-24%. Energy Regen +10-20% when HP > 80%. Best standard 5-star sword, great on any DPS.

Weapon Pull Priority

  • Signature weapons are ~15-25% better than standard 5-star alternatives. Pull if you main that character.
  • Standard 5-star weapons from the permanent banner are excellent for F2P - especially Emerald of Genesis and Static Mist.
  • 4-star weapons at R5 can match R1 standard 5-stars. Prioritize refining craftable weapons over pulling.
  • CRIT weapons (CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG secondary stat) are universally better than ATK% weapons for DPS characters.
