About WuWa Tools

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Wuthering Waves?

Wuthering Waves is an open-world action RPG developed by Kuro Games. Set in a post-apocalyptic world, players take on the role of a Rover who awakens with the ability to absorb the powers of defeated enemies (Echoes). The game features fast-paced real-time combat, a gacha system for acquiring Resonators (characters) and weapons, and a vast world to explore with puzzles, challenges, and world bosses.

What tools does WuWa Tools offer?

WuWa Tools provides a complete suite of free resources for Wuthering Waves players: active redemption codes updated in real-time, a community-driven tier list ranking all Resonators, detailed character build guides with optimal echo sets and weapon choices, a weapon database with stat comparisons, an echo database with set bonuses and main stat recommendations, a daily checklist for waveplate and commission tracking, and an event/banner calendar.

How do I redeem Wuthering Waves codes?

To redeem codes in Wuthering Waves, open the in-game menu (ESC), go to Settings > Other > Redeem Code, enter your code, and confirm. You can also use the official redemption website. Codes typically expire within 1-2 patches, so redeem them as soon as they're announced. Our codes page is updated live with all working codes.

Is WuWa Tools affiliated with Kuro Games?

No. WuWa Tools is a fan-made community resource and is not officially affiliated with or endorsed by Kuro Games. All game content, images, and trademarks belong to their respective owners.
