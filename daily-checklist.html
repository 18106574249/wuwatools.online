Daily Routine Tips

  • Do commissions first - they take 5 minutes and give 60 Astrite guaranteed.
  • Spend waveplates on what you need most - echoes for builds, forgery for weapon mats, bosses for ascension.
  • Tower of Adversity resets every 2 weeks - check it daily but don't stress if you can't clear all floors.
  • Weekly bosses reset Monday 4:00 AM - plan your 3 weekly clears around your schedule.
  • Save waveplates before new patches - new characters often need new boss materials.
