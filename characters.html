WuWa Resonator Tier List - Builds & Best Teams

Every Resonator ranked with optimal builds, weapon choices, echo sets, and team synergy analysis. Updated for version 3.1.

Choosing the right Resonator and build can make or break your Tower of Adversity clears. This guide ranks every character by their overall performance across all endgame content - not just raw damage, but also team utility, ease of use, and future-proof investment value. Each entry includes the optimal echo set, signature weapon, and recommended teammates.

S-Tier - Meta Defining

These Resonators dominate the current meta. They excel in Tower of Adversity, Hologram VI, and all endgame modes with minimal investment.

Jinhsi

Spectro DPS - Celestial Light 5pc - Ages of Harvest

Jinhsi's Incarnation state delivers the highest burst damage in the game. Her Forte Circuit charges quickly with coordinated attacks, and her Resonance Liberation can one-shot most elite enemies. Pair with Yinlin or Zhezhi for coordinated attack triggers. Best team: Jinhsi + Yinlin + Shorekeeper.

Camellya

Havoc DPS - Sun-sinking Eclipse 5pc - Red Spring

Camellya's Budding Mode transforms her basic attacks into wide AoE havoc slashes with self-healing. Her damage scales with HP loss, making her incredibly tanky in prolonged fights. Best team: Camellya + Roccia + Shorekeeper. Excels in both single-target and AoE scenarios.

Shorekeeper

Spectro Support - Rejuvenating Glow 5pc - Stellar Symphony

The best universal support in the game. Shorekeeper provides team-wide healing, damage amplification, and energy regeneration. Her Stellarealm creates a field that buffs all allies inside it. Fits any team. Priority pull for new players.

Roccia

Havoc Sub-DPS - Sun-sinking Eclipse 5pc - Deep Sea

Roccia's off-field Havoc application and RES shred make her the ideal partner for Camellya. Her Ultimate provides grouping and significant Havoc DMG bonus. Best team: Camellya + Roccia + Shorekeeper.

A-Tier - Strong & Reliable

Excellent performers that can clear all content. Slightly lower ceiling than S-Tier but still top-tier investments.

Changli

Fusion DPS - Molten Rift 5pc - Blazing Brilliance

Changli's True Sight state grants massive Fusion DMG bonus and interrupt resistance. Her charged attacks during True Sight deal devastating single-target damage. Best team: Changli + Brant + Shorekeeper. Requires some skill to manage Forte stacks.

Xiangli Yao

Electro DPS - Void Thunder 5pc - Verity's Handle

Xiangli Yao's Mechanismo summons deal sustained Electro damage while he attacks from range. His Ultimate provides a massive ATK buff to the entire team. Best team: Xiangli Yao + Yinlin + Verina. Great for players who prefer ranged combat.

Zhezhi

Glacio Sub-DPS - Freezing Frost 5pc - Rime-Draped Sprouts

Zhezhi's Ink Spirit provides consistent off-field Glacio damage and grouping. Her Outro Skill buffs the next character's Glacio DMG significantly. Best team: Any Glacio DPS + Zhezhi + Shorekeeper.

Verina

Spectro Healer - Rejuvenating Glow 5pc - Variation

The best F2P-friendly healer. Verina provides consistent team healing, ATK buffs, and can use the 4-star weapon Variation for energy. Best team: Universal healer slot. Use her if you don't have Shorekeeper.

B-Tier - Solid Choices

Viable for all content but require more investment or specific team compositions to match higher tiers.

Brant

Fusion DPS - Molten Rift 5pc - Unflickering Valor

Brant's Anchor Mode provides high burst damage but requires careful positioning. His damage scales with DEF, making him unusually tanky. Best team: Brant + Changli + Shorekeeper.

Phoebe

Spectro DPS - Celestial Light 5pc - Luminous Hymn

Phoebe's Light Weaver state enables rapid Spectro attacks with self-buffs. Strong in single-target but struggles in AoE without grouping support. Best team: Phoebe + Jinhsi + Shorekeeper for double Spectro synergy.

Encore

Fusion DPS - Molten Rift 5pc - Cosmic Ripples

Encore's Dissonance mechanic rewards aggressive play with escalating Fusion DMG. High skill ceiling but devastating when mastered. Best team: Encore + Changli + Verina.

Sanhua

Glacio Sub-DPS - Freezing Frost 5pc - Emerald of Genesis

Sanhua's Ice Lotus provides excellent off-field Glacio application and her Outro buffs Basic Attack DMG. Best team: Any Basic Attack-focused DPS + Sanhua + healer. Great F2P sub-DPS option.

Build Priority Tips

  • New players: Pull Shorekeeper first - she fits every team and makes all content easier.
  • Echo main stats: DPS want CRIT Rate/DMG 4-cost, Element DMG 3-cost, ATK% 1-cost. Supports want HP%/Healing Bonus.
  • Weapon priority: Signature 5-star > Standard 5-star > Battle Pass 4-star > Craftable 4-star.
  • Team building: Always run 1 Main DPS + 1 Sub-DPS/Support + 1 Healer. Double DPS teams work but need high investment.
