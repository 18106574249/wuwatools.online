Wuthering Waves Banner Schedule (2026) - Current & Upcoming

Current and upcoming convene banners with pity system guide. Version 3.1.

Convene banners are the gacha system in Wuthering Waves. Each version runs for approximately 6 weeks with two banner phases. The pity system guarantees a 5-star character at 80 pulls (hard pity), with a 50/50 chance for the rate-up character. Losing the 50/50 guarantees the next 5-star is the rate-up character.

Pity System Guide

  • Character banner: 80 pulls hard pity. Soft pity starts at pull 65 (increased 5-star rate).
  • Weapon banner: 80 pulls hard pity. 75% chance for rate-up weapon (better odds than character banner).
  • Standard banner: 80 pulls hard pity. Uses standard pulls (Lustrous Tides), not premium pulls.
  • Pity carries over between banners of the same type. Your pull count resets when you get a 5-star.
  • 50/50 system: First 5-star on character banner has 50% chance to be the rate-up. Losing guarantees next is rate-up.
